Privacy Policy Of Plantsulin


Here at Plantsulin, we value the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy details the procedures by which we gather and utilize information from individuals who visit our website.

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please email us.

Log Files

Log files are utilized for monitoring visitor details like IP addresses, browser types, and visiting times. This information aids in analyzing patterns and maintaining the website without disclosing the personal identities of visitors.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Our website utilizes cookies to save data on your preferences and the pages you browse. This enables us to improve your online experience through customized content.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Our website uses Google's DART cookies to showcase advertisements that align with your browsing activities on our website and others. If you wish to disable these cookies, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy available at Google Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Advertisers

Cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons might be utilized by third-party ad networks for ad delivery and performance tracking. Please note that we do not have authority over these cookies.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

Please note that our Privacy Policy pertains solely to our website and does not extend to other websites or advertisers. For details on how your data is managed by them, we recommend reviewing their respective Privacy Policies. You can access links to these policies by visiting Privacy Policy Links.

Cookies Management

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. For guidance on how to disable cookies, refer to your browser’s website.

Children’s Privacy

Our dedication lies in safeguarding the online privacy of children. We ensure that no personal information is intentionally gathered from individuals under the age of 13. If you suspect that your child has shared such details, kindly reach out to us so we can promptly remove it.

Online Privacy Policy Only

The Privacy Policy pertains specifically to data obtained online via our website and does not extend to offline information or data collected through different mediums.

Consent

By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy and its terms.
